4 Days, 3 Nights Sipi Falls Tour

Visit one of the most beautiful waterfalls in Uganda! The Sipi Falls are located in Kapchorwa District just next to Mount Elgon National Park. This safari will let you tour the falls plus its surroundings in three days of travel. It will let you create everlasting memories in your life about this particular safari, an experience you will always remember in the future and smile to the fact that you were there and you took photographs around too. Budget and Moderate accommodation facilities are recommended but a luxury experience will make you feel much better hence do not hesitate to spend enough dollars on accommodation if you can. It can only make your safari better.

Day 1 – Transfer to Sipi Falls
Wake up to a very early morning breakfast because you have to set off by 0600 hrs and rive to eastern Uganda a journey that should take you approximately 5 hours by road. You will drive through Kampala, Mabira, Jinja, Iganga and proceed to Mbale where you should arrive by noon. You can choose to have early lunch and head straight for the adventurous Sipi Falls hike an activity that should last for about 4 hours depending on your fitness level or alternatively first carry out the activity and then have lunch as you relax at your lodge. Overnight at Sipi River Lodge or Lacam Lodge. Meal Plan; Lunch and Dinner.

Day 2 – Caves Tour Community Tour and Coffee Tour
This day you will spend majority of your morning hours touring various caves around Mount Elgon National Park whilst learning about their respective facts and history. Your guide will also take you to the smallest falls for another breathtaking sight of the falls. After lunch, you will head for a community tour where you should meet various members of the community and see them carry out their day to day activities. You will also carry out a coffee plantations tour where you will learn how to plant and process Arabic coffee before you retreat for the activity and return to the lodge for dinner and overnight. Overnight at Sipi River Lodge or Lacam Lodge. Meal Plan; Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

Day 3 – Jinja Tour
This day you will have an early morning set off from Kapchorwa and head to Jinja where you will visit the source of the Nile and the John Speke Monument in Bukaya as well. You will also visit the first and oldest bridge in Uganda that was built by the Indian Coolies decades ago. You will spend your evening at The Boubon in Jinja before driving to King Fisher Safaris Resort for an overnight. Overnight at King Fisher Safaris Resort in Bukaya.

Day 4 – Transfer to Kampala and Departure
This day you have an early morning breakfast and then head for a morning boat cruise on River Nile. After this activity we shall set off for our Journey to Kampala making a stopover at the Sezibwa Falls for another scenic tour before making our final journey to Kampala for a late dropoff
