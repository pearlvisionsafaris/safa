3 Days Kidepo Fly In Safari

Kidepo Valley National Park is apparently the most isolated National Park in Uganda lying far north in the wild frontier region of Uganda. This 3 days safari will offer you the best safari moments in this top African wildlife viewing destination in Uganda. To cut down on the distance, this safari features chartered flights to and fro hence, you will not get fatigued travelling like you would have done with the road option.

Day 1 – Transfer to Kidepo Valley National Park
Shortly after your breakfast, our safari guide will pick you up from your hotel and drop you to Entebbe International Airport to catch up with your chartered flight to Kidepo Valley National Park. The flight sets off at exactly 1230 hrs hence you ought to make it there in time so as to avoid any delays. You should reach Kidepo at 1400 hrs, check in at your lodge for lunch and a slight relaxation then head out for an evening game drive at the Naurus Valley where you should sight Elephants, Cape Buffaloes, Giraffes, Zebras plus more species of wildlife. Overnight at Apoka Lodge for luxury, Kidepo Savannah Lodge for Mid-range and Apoka Rest Camp for budget. Meal Plan: Lunch and Dinner.

Day 2 – Wildlife Viewing in Kidepo
This day we head for a morning fame drive around the park for another exciting wildlife viewing moment that should last for 3 to 4 hours. You will return to the lodge for lunch and then head for an afternoon cultural experience with the Karamojongs at Karena and Kapedo. Overnight at Apoka Lodge for luxury, Kidepo Savannah Lodge for Mid-range and Apoka Rest Camp for budget.

Day 3 – Transfer to Kampala and Departure
This day you will have breakfast and take a nature walk around the park under the accompaniment of an armed game ranger for protection. After this walk, you will return to your lodge for lunch and then catch up with your return flight to Entebbe as you wait for your flight back home. Meal Plan: Breakfast and Lunch
