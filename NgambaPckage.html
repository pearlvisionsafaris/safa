3 Days Ngamba Chimpanzee Tracking

Ngamba Island Chimpanzee Sanctuary is one of the Chimpanzee habitats in Uganda. This island is approximately 23 Kilometers away from Entebbe and can only be accessed through water transport. This island shelters about 42 rescued orphaned Chimpanzees that you will see when you embark on this tour.

Day 1 – Transfer to Ngamba Island
After having breakfast in the morning, you will be met by our safari guide and driver who should drop you at the landing site to catch with a boat to Ngamba. You will proceed to the island with the guide where you should arrive in a few hours, have lunch and proceed for a Chimpanzee tracking experience. Please note that this is your chance to care for these orphaned Chimpanzees hence, feel free to throw some fruits towards them as you watch them play in their natural habitats. Overnight at Ngamba Island Camp. Meal plan: Lunch and Dinner.

Day 2 – Ngamba Island Forest Walk
Rise to an early morning breakfast and head for a thrilling forest walk. Please ensure that you have taken the necessary vaccinations before engaging in this activity because you are bound to get real close to these chimps that some of them will pull your clothes, shake your hands or even climb on your neck. You will then return to the lodge for lunch and relaxation at the lodge or rather opt for an afternoon nature walk around the Island. Overnight at Ngamba Island Camp. Meal plan: Lunch and Dinner.

Day 3 – Transfer back to Entebbe
This day you transfer back to Entebbe where you will choose to either visit the Uganda Wildlife Education Center (UWEC) or have a tour around Entebbe Botanical Gardens before you are dropped off either at the Airport for departure or around Entebbe for an overnight.
